Connect to Notion

To connect your Notion workspace, you'll need an Internal Integration Token.

How to get your token:

  1. Go to notion.com/my-integrations
  2. Click "New integration"
  3. Give it a name (e.g., "WordPress Sync")
  4. Copy the "Internal Integration Token"
  5. Share your Notion pages with the integration
Your token starts with "secret_" and should be kept confidential.
This will verify your token and establish the connection.
